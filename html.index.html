The Ascent from Shadow

**Crimson Gloom** is the sound of a heart breaking on a snow-capped peak. Formed in the high shadows where eternal **love** meets the cold finality of **death**, the band channels the operatic despair of Nightwish and the doom-laden resonance of Type O Negative.

Our music is a journey through the granite of ancient **mountains**, overgrown **cemeteries**, and the rivers of **blood** that flow between life and eternity. Led by the ethereal vocals of *Lady Isolde*, we invite you to embrace the beautiful gloom that rests upon the world.
